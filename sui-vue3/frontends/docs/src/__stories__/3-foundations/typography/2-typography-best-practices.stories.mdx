## Accessibility

<table>
  <thead>
    <tr>
      <th>Do's</th>
      <th>Don'ts</th>
    </tr>
  </thead>
  <tbody>
    <tr>
      <td>
        ✅ For the majority of use cases, especially long-form, utilize a font size of 15px or higher. For less critical information, smaller fonts are acceptable,
        but they should only be used occasionally. This guarantees that all of our users, including those with low vision, can access the material.
        <br/><br/>
        <img src={accessibilityDo1} alt="Typography do sample" width="406" />
      </td>
      <td>
        ❌ Don't use font size below 15px for body copy and UI, unless the content is brief or secondary.
        <br/><br/>
        <img src={accessibilityDont1} alt="Typography don't sample" width="406" />
      </td>
    </tr>
    <tr>
      <td>
        ✅ Maintain a logical hierarchy by selecting headings in numerical order rather than font size.
        This improves the accessibility of our contents for screen readers.
        <br/><br/>
        <img src={accessibilityDo2} alt="Hierarchy do sample" width="406" />
      </td>
      <td>
        ❌ Use headings in an illogical order, since this will confuse screen readers.
        <br/><br/>
        <img src={accessibilityDont2} alt="Hierarchy don't sample" width="406" />
      </td>
    </tr>
  </tbody>
</table>


## Style

<table>
  <thead>
    <tr>
      <th>Do's</th>
      <th>Don'ts</th>
    </tr>
  </thead>
  <tbody>
    <tr>
      <td>
        ✅ Text should be aligned, spaced, and styled to make it simple to read and understand what to do.
        <br/><br/>
        <img src={styleDo1} alt="Style do sample with normal alignment" width="406" />
      </td>
      <td>
        ❌ Use of unreadable colors, tight leading, underlined text that can be misinterpreted for a link, and center alignment of content.
        <br/><br/>
        <img src={styleDont1} alt="Style don't sample with center alignment" width="406" />
      </td>
    </tr>
    <tr>
      <td>
        ✅ On titles, headers, and subheadings, use sentence case.
        <br/><br/>
        <img src={styleDo2} alt="Style do sample with normal casing" width="406" />
      </td>
      <td>
        ❌ Use all caps on titles, headers, and subheading.
        <br/><br/>
        <img src={styleDont2} alt="Style don't sample with capitalized casing" width="406" />
      </td>
    </tr>
  </tbody>
</table>
