## Visual rhythm

In order to reduce cognitive load on our users, and ensure everything we design and build is clear and easy to understand, a visual rhythm (or spacing)
rule should be established that falls within the 8px baseline grid, i.e. it should be a multiple of 8.

### Global elements

- The sm-container has a **max-width of 1140px** and is centre aligned, unless it is set at full-width which is only used on certain pages with very wide, data heavy tables.
- The sm-container should have **&gt;1366 padding: 48px 56px** (48 top and bottom, 56 left and right).
- The sm-container should have **&lt;1367 padding: 48px 32px**.
- The margin under the page title should be **40px**.
- If you are using filters, ensure the space below is **24px**.

### Page content: large

Spacing between stacked content should be **24px**. An example of large content would be stacked cards that are roughly 80â€“100px+ in height, although this may vary depending on the content.

### Page spacing: small

Spacing between small stacked content should be **16px**. An example of small content would be stacked cards that are roughly 72px or less, in height.

<img src={pageContentSmall} alt="Small page content sample" />

### Content spacing
- Spacing between sections and around content is **24px** for medium components
- Spacing between sections and around content is **16px** for small components
- Spacing between sections and around content is **56/48px** for large components (see wizard below). Using your own spatial judgement is necessary here as well.
