## Usage
- When there are many items to display and the user only needs to focus on a few at a time, use this option.
- Use for related items only. Items should all have a similar theme.
- Do not use for non-visual items like links or paragraphs.

## Formatting
### Anatomy
**1: Navigation buttons -**  Controls for moving between slides.

**2: Slide Content -** Slides can be images, videos, or other types of information.

## Behaviour
### States
Navigational elements such as arrows and close buttons can be clicked and follow the states of the **icon button component.**
<img src={behaviorStates1} className="img-padding" alt="Arrows and close buttons can be clicked and follow the states of the icon button component" />

Image content **on hover** has a **1px border**, and shows the close button (optional).
<img src={behaviorStates2} className="img-padding" alt="Image content on hover has a 1px border, and shows the close button" />

### Navigation
Content slider items can be navigated in 3 ways:
1. Button icons (arrows) - Buttons should disappear when there are no longer any images to scroll through.

2. Swipe motion (all touch devices)

3. Tab and arrow keys on the keyboard
