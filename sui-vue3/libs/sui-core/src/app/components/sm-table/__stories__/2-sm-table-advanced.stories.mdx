## Advanced table

he advanced table is used in SiteMinder for our CM product where users are required to create and map out the rooms, rates, and rate plans for their property. Much of the design has been carried over from the old system and replicated in our new product, so therefore it's a somewhat bespoke table layout.

This SUI component is a skeleton table for product teams to build up on. We provide the 3-level dropdown layout only, anything inside and beyond this falls into custom development for each product. For example, anything inside the cells is considered custom content - from the icons, input fields, buttons etc.

Sizes follow the default sizes for the standard table. (xsmall, small, medium, large, xlarge)

## Behaviours

### Expanding and collapsing rows
Rows can be expanded and collapsed by clicking on the chevron to the left of the row. The information that displays in expanded form has a slightly different
background colour and is connected to the chevron visually to emphasis that the expanded section is part of the original table row.
