## Usage

### What is the sm-user-list?
The sm-user-list is presented as a list item within the panel in the sm-app-header. It contains essential details like the notification message, timestamp, and an icon or indicator.

#### Key usages:
1. **Immediate Alerts:** Notify users about important events right away, such as system alerts, security warnings, or critical updates.

2. **Management:** Allow users to mark notifications as read/unread, delete, or archive them to keep their notification list organized.

3. **Announcements:** Send important updates or news to all users, such as maintenance schedules or new features.

4. **Status Updates:** Inform users about the progress of ongoing processes or requests.


## Formatting

### Anatomy
**1: Read/Unread Status** - Differentiation between new (unread) and old (read) notifications.

**2: Title** - The title of the notification message.

**3: Subtext** - The content of the notification, often truncated when message is long.

**4: Timestamp** - Indicates when the notification was received or when the event occurred.

## Responsiveness
On mobile and tablet, for long titles, the sm-user-list has a maximum of 2 lines before it truncates. This is the same for the subtext. Timestamp has a maximum width of 80px, maximum of 1 line before it truncates.
